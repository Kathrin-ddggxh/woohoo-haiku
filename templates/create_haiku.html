Write your own Haiku

Writing a haiku is simple and with some practice a lot of fun. Just follow this basic structure as the minimal rule set, everything else is up to you!

Use three lines for your haiku.

The first and third line have 5 syllables each

Your haiku shouldn't rhyme

Traditionally a cutting word, called a kireji is included as well as a seasonal reference. In this modern however not all topics warrent a seasonal reference and in order to make easier for beginners a kireji is not mandetory.

Just tag your haiku with one of the subject provided and don't worry about anything else. Have fun!
